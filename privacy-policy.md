# Privacy Policy for GhostBlock

**Last Updated:** November 17, 2025

## Our Privacy Commitment

GhostBlock is designed with privacy as the top priority. We believe you should have complete control over your data and browsing experience without any surveillance or tracking.

## Data Collection

**We do NOT collect any personal data.** GhostBlock:
- Does not collect information about you
- Does not track your browsing habits
- Does not analyze which websites you visit
- Does not build a profile of your interests
- Does not store any identifying information

## What Data We Store

The only data Element Blocker stores is:
- **CSS Selectors** of elements you choose to block
- **Website domain names** (hostname only) associated with your blocks
- This data is stored **exclusively on your device** in your local Chrome storage

## Storage Details

- **Location**: Chrome's `chrome.storage.local` API (encrypted on your device)
- **Accessibility**: Only GhostBlock can access this data
- **Deletion**: All data is permanently deleted if you uninstall the extension
- **Backup**: Your data is NOT synced to Google's servers or any cloud service

## No Network Requests

GhostBlock does NOT:
- Send any data to external servers
- Make any network requests (except to load the website's content you visit)
- Communicate with any third-party services
- Use analytics, tracking pixels, or telemetry

## No Cookies or Analytics

- No cookies are set by this extension
- No analytics services are used
- No user tracking occurs
- No behavioral data is collected

## How We Use Your Data

The only data we store locally is used for:
1. Remembering which elements you've chosen to block
2. Re-applying blocks when you reload a page
3. Displaying your blocks in the management interface

## Third-Party Services

GhostBlock does NOT use:
- Google Analytics
- Mixpanel or similar analytics
- Advertising networks
- Data brokers
- Any third-party libraries that collect data

## Security

- All stored data is local to your device
- Chrome handles encryption of stored data
- No data transmission means no interception risk
- Your browsing behavior remains private

## Your Rights

You have complete control:
- **View**: Access all your blocked elements anytime
- **Delete**: Remove individual blocks or clear all blocks
- **Export**: Your data is yours - stored on your device in plain text
- **Uninstall**: All data is deleted when you uninstall the extension

## Data Retention

- Data is retained only as long as you use the extension
- Uninstalling the extension deletes all associated data
- Clearing Chrome storage clears all extension data

## Children's Privacy

Element Blocker does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. Parents and guardians should teach children about responsible internet use.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy occasionally. We will notify users of significant changes by:
- Updating the "Last Updated" date
- Notifying through the Chrome Web Store

## Questions About Privacy

If you have any questions about our privacy practices:
- Review this policy thoroughly
- Check the extension's manifest.json for permissions
- Inspect the source code (available for review)

## Manifest Permissions Explained

The extension requests these permissions:
- **activeTab**: To interact with the currently active tab
- **storage**: To save your blocked elements locally
- **scripting**: To inject scripts that hide blocked elements
- **host_permissions** (`*://*/*`): To work on any website you visit

These are necessary for the extension's core functionality and do NOT enable data collection.

## Compliance

GhostBlock complies with:
- Chrome Web Store privacy requirements
- GDPR principles (though no personal data is collected)
- CCPA standards (though no personal data is collected)
- General privacy best practices

## Our Transparency

This extension is built with:
- No hidden trackers
- No obfuscated code
- Transparent permissions
- Clear privacy practices

We believe privacy is a fundamental right and have designed GhostBlock accordingly.

**In Summary**: GhostBlock stores only your block choices locally on your device, shares nothing with anyone, and collects no data about you. Your privacy is guaranteed. for Element Blocker
